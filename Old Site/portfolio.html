Client Name

Lifecare Ventures Limited

Industry

Manufacturing ( Agro- Allied)

Description

Wireless Network Expansion, Email and Collaboration Server Solution

November 2005 – still on-going

Challenge: Lifecare had several challenges with their existing wireless network, which was very erratic and problems with viruses which were causing disruptions to system use. They also needed to extend the coverage of the network to cover their entire factory and a more efficient way to send information than paper based memos.

What We Did: We implemented Symantec Client Security, network security software which detects and eliminates viruses, also preventing them from spreading to affect other systems on the network. We supervised the installation of a new wireless router and extended its signal coverage using a Linksys Range Expander. We also deployed an effective Email and collaboration suite, Kerio Mail server which runs on a Dell PoweEdge 800 server which allows them to send electronic mail, share documents (reports, design templates) and work together on projects without leaving their desks.
                                                                                                                       

Project Benefits

Increased productivity, as staff can now work effectively without the frustration of lost files and system damage caused by viruses

More efficient , cheaper information exchange as there are fewer paper memos passed around

Because managers have better access to information, they can make informed decisions faster and be more effective at their jobs

Client Name

Loral International  Schools Organisation

Industry

Education (primary, secondary and pre- tertiary)

Project Description

Website Restructuring, Network Restructuring , IT awareness Training

Jun. 2005 – still on-going

Challenge: Loral Schools had several problems with their website as they were getting complaints from parents in Nigeria and abroad that it was slow to load and in some cases did not open. This was very embarrassing for the school after investing heavily in the site’s design and hosting. They also had an abnormally slow network and internet connection

What We Did: We conducted an audit of the site and found several problems, which we advised them on ;enabling them get their original website consultant to correct those problems ,which included irrelevant references to other sites, wrong spellings among others,  and eventually moved their website hosting to another faster website hosting service
We also conducted a survey of their physical network and found several problems in the cabling done originally, which was responsible for terminal damage to several computers and networking switches.
We replaced the defective cabling, switches and supplied several new computers to bolster the amount of computers available for students and staff. We also worked on enlightening of the management staff on the importance of Information Technology, and how beneficial it could be to their activities

We’ve provided access to bulk sms messaging which enables them send out information more cheaply and more effectively to their staff, parents and the general public.
                                                                                                                     

Project Benefits

Website is now up and running , with visitors from Nigeria and beyond requesting  and receiving information about the school
More efficient network  and faster internet access
